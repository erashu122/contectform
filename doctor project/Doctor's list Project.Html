• Our Doctors

    • Dr. Linda Malson

      Dr. Linda Malson is a board-certified dermatologist. She graduated magna cum laude from Brown University with a Bachelor of Science in Biology and Community Health. She returned to Brown to complete her medical education at the Warren Alpert Medical School where she was inducted into the prestigious Alpha Omega Alpha Honor Medical Society. She then completed a rigorous Internal Medicine Internship at the Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine followed by her Dermatology Residency at Howard University. In her free time, Dr. Malson enjoys going for long walks with her two golden retrievers and boating on the lake and river on the weekends.

    • Dr. Eny Queatan

      Dr. Eny Queatan is a board-certified dermatologist joining IDI from her practice at NorthShore University Health System. Born and raised in Highland Park, Illinois, Dr. Eny found her passion for medicine and dermatology while serving as a clinical dermatology assistant prior to college. At the University of Wisconsin in Madison, she graduated with distinction, then pursued her medical training at the Chicago Medical School at Rosalind Franklin University of Medicine and Science. After medical school, Dr. Queatan completed her general medicine internship at Advocate Lutheran General Hospital. She then completed her residency in dermatology at SUNY Downstate in Brooklyn, NY. Dr. Eny enjoys caring for patients with a variety of dermatologic conditions. She values a thorough approach, working closely with patients to achieve the best clinical outcomes.
